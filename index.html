About Me:

I'm a first year astronomy graduate student and a McCarthy-Stoeger Vatican Observatory fellow at Steward Observatory . My main area of research is galaxy formation theory, with emphasis in the Local Group dwarfs gallaxies and modelling Lyman alpha emitter galaxies. You can have a look at my CV and my publications ADS. I earn my Masters in physics from Universidad de los Andes, Bogota, Colombia in 2015. There I worked with professor Jaime E. Forero-Romero. I earn my B.S in physics at Universidad Nacional de Colombia, Bogota in 2013.
