About Me:

I'm a second year physics master student working with Jaime E. Forero-Romero at Universidad de los Andes in Bogotá, Colombia. My main research interest is in galaxy formation theory. In particular I'm interested in two areas: Devoloping new models that help us to understand the main characteristics of high redshift galaxies. Studying the Local Group dwarf galaxies in order to understand galaxy formation processes. I'm also interested in applying machine learning and data science methods in astronomy. You can find my CV here and my publications ADS. All my work and codes that I have developed are public available in my github github page.
