Hunting for the Dark Matter wake induced by the LMC.

The LMC being the most massive satellite of the Milky Way and in it's first passage around the LMC it most generate the strongest Dark Matter Wake in the Milky Way. In this paper, we quantified the density enhance and kinematic perturbation produced by the Dark Matter Wake induced by the LMC. Paper figures can be found here [Paper] .
