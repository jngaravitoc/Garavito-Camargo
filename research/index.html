The interaction of the Milky Way and the Large Magellanic Cloud:

Here I show some complemenatry figures from my paper Hunting for the Dark Matter Wake Indcued by the Large Magellanic Cloud (LMC). The figures bellow show the perturbations cause by the LMC in the density, velocity dispersion, and mean volocities of the stellar halo of the Milky Way. The figures show spherical projections in mollweide coordiantes at different galactocentric radii.
